Resources for Students

Guides, tools, and opportunities to help you thrive in your studies and beyond.

Tips to Improve Your Prompts

To get the best results from GenAI, keep these principles in mind when asking questions:

  • Be Clear and Specific
    Explain the process of photosynthesis in simple terms for a biology exam.
  • Provide Context
    I need to prepare for my calculus test on integrals. What are the most important techniques to practice?
  • Ask Follow-up Questions
    Can you give a step-by-step example?
  • Specify Language or Style
    Explain in simple English suitable for a university student.
  • Specify Length
    Give a short summary of 200 words.
  • Specify Format
    Provide the answer as bullet points or a table.
  • Combined Example
    Write a 300-word summary of the causes of World War I in bullet points for a second-year university student in clear English.

Practical Prompts for Students

Here are ways you can use Generative AI to improve your studies and productivity:

Study and Revision:

  • Summarize Notes
    Summarize my lecture notes into a concise one-page study guide.
  • Explain Difficult Concepts
    Explain the concept of thermodynamics with examples suitable for a first-year student.
  • Create Practice Questions
    Generate 10 practice questions on linear algebra with answers.
  • Check Understanding
    Test my understanding of the chapter by asking 5 short-answer questions.

Writing and Assignments:

  • Brainstorm Ideas
    Give 5 essay topic ideas on climate change for university students.
  • Improve Drafts
    Check my essay draft and suggest ways to improve clarity and structure.
  • Create Outlines
    Make a detailed outline for a research paper on AI ethics.

Productivity:

  • Plan Study Sessions
    Help me create a weekly study schedule for my exams.
  • Summarize Articles or PDFs
    Summarize this research paper in simple terms highlighting the main points.
  • Language Practice
    Correct my English writing and explain the grammar mistakes.

Generative AI is a powerful study tool, but it should support your learning, not replace your own thinking. Always critically evaluate the output and double-check important information with trusted sources.
