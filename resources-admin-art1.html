AI for Students

Boost your learning, enhance productivity, and maintain academic integrity.

Learning with AI

AI tools like ChatGPT can help you summarise complex readings, break down difficult topics, and generate study questions. Use AI to explain concepts in simpler terms and provide examples, but always verify the accuracy.

Using AI in Assignments

  • Use AI to brainstorm essay outlines, but write your own final draft.
  • Ask AI for alternative phrasings to improve clarity.
  • Proofread your work for grammar and spelling.
  • Follow your institution’s plagiarism policy and cite AI-generated ideas when required.

Example Prompts

  • “Summarise the key points of this [paste text] in under 200 words.”
  • “Explain [topic] in simple terms suitable for a high school student.”
  • “Generate 5 practice quiz questions on [subject].”
  • “Help me create a revision timetable for [exam name].”

Recommended Tools

Besides ChatGPT, you can use:

  • Perplexity.ai — for AI-assisted search with citations.
  • Grammarly — grammar and clarity improvements.
  • Notion AI — AI-assisted note-taking.
