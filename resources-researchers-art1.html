Literature Reviews

Use AI to summarise large volumes of research papers, extract key themes, and generate annotated bibliographies. Always verify sources and check for bias.

Data Analysis

  • Generate scripts for statistical analysis in Python, R, or MATLAB.
  • Ask AI to explain statistical models and suggest suitable tests for your data.

Research Writing

  • Draft abstracts or plain-language summaries for wider audiences.
  • Generate alternative phrasing to improve clarity.
  • Use AI to check grammar and adherence to style guidelines.
